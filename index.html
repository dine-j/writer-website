About Chloe

✦

Chloe Mandel writes dark and gothic fantasy where shadows breathe and beauty is laced with dread. Her stories explore the thin membrane between the world we know and the one that watches us from the dark.

When not writing, she can be found in old bookshops, candlelit rooms, and places where the walls feel just a little too alive.

"The most honest stories are the ones told in the dark."

Chloe Mandel
